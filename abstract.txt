Women safety is a major concern in today’s society, especially during travel and late working hours. Many existing solutions depend on smartphones, which may not always be accessible during emergencies. This project proposes a smart safety wrist watch that looks like a normal watch but includes a hidden emergency system. On pressing a single button, the device sends emergency calls and SMS with live location to the nearest police station, family members, and friends, while also activating a loud siren to attract nearby attention. The system is compact, wearable, easy to use, and designed for real-world deployment.
